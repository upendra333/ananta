Our Story

ANANTA MOBILITY was founded with a vision to revolutionize the electric mobility landscape in India. We recognized the growing need for reliable, sustainable transportation solutions that could serve both individual consumers and businesses.

Starting as a small team with big dreams, we have grown into a comprehensive electric mobility solutions provider, serving customers across multiple states and partnering with leading e-commerce platforms.

  • Founded in 2020 with a vision for sustainable mobility
  • Comprehensive EV solutions for retail and business
  • Strong partnerships with Bigbasket and Flipkart
  • 100+ electric vehicles in our fleet

Our Values

Our core values guide everything we do, from customer service to environmental responsibility. These principles shape our culture and drive our commitment to excellence.

  • Sustainability - Committed to reducing carbon footprint through electric mobility
  • Reliability - Ensuring dependable service and support for all our customers
  • Excellence - Maintaining high standards in all our operations and services
  • Innovation - Continuously improving our solutions and services
